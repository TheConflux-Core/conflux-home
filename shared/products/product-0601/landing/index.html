Frequently Asked Questions

What is CMMC 2.0 Level 2?

CMMC 2.0 Level 2 requires implementing all 110 security requirements from NIST SP 800-171. It is mandatory for any contractor handling Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) on DoD contracts.

When is the CMMC deadline?

CMMC 2.0 Level 2 becomes a requirement in all new DoD contracts by October 31, 2026. Existing contracts will be phased in over time.

Is CMMC Shield a substitute for a C3PAO assessment?

No. CMMC Shield helps you prepare for the C3PAO assessment by identifying gaps, generating required documents, and tracking remediation. You still need a certified C3PAO to conduct the formal assessment.

How long does the gap assessment take?

Most small contractors complete the assessment in 2-4 hours. You can save progress and return at any time.

Can I export my SSP and POA&M?

Yes. Both documents are generated in Markdown format and can be exported as PDF. They follow standard CMMC documentation formats accepted by C3PAOs.
