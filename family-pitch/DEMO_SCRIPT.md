# THE CONFLUX — Demo Script (5-Minute Family Presentation)

---

## Setup

- Screen share or in-person laptop demo
- Have the app running and signed in
- Browser open to theconflux.com on second tab/window

---

## Script

### Opening (30 seconds)

> "I want to show you something I've been building. This isn't a website or a concept — it's a real, working application. Give me five minutes."

---

### Part 1: The Product (2 minutes)

**1. Launch the app**
> "This is Conflux Home. When you download it, you sign in, and it asks you a few questions about what you need help with."

**2. Show onboarding**
> "Based on your answers, it builds you a team of AI agents. Each one has a name, a personality, and a specialty."

*Walk through: Name entry → Conversation → Team selection → Alive animation*

**3. Land on the desktop**
> "And this is your home. You've got a team of AI agents ready to work, 16 built-in apps, and everything connects."

*Quick tour: Click through 3-4 apps (Budget, Kitchen, Studio, Chat)*

**4. Show agent chat**
> "Watch this — each agent thinks differently. Conflux is strategic. Helix does research. Forge builds things. They remember everything and work together."

*Send a message, show the response*

---

### Part 2: The Numbers (1 minute)

**5. Pull up the one-pager (or financials)**
> "Here's what matters: The AI agent market is $180 billion and growing. We have a working product. Most startups in this space are still building prototypes. We have 16 apps, user accounts, billing, and cloud infrastructure."

*Quick numbers:*
- "3 paying customers covers all our costs"
- "350 customers is $8,700/month revenue"
- "There's $2 million in grant money we can apply for"

---

### Part 3: The Ask (30 seconds)

> "I'm not asking for money. I'm asking for three things:"
> 1. "Belief — look at what's built. This is real."
> 2. "Support — emotional support while I push this to launch."
> 3. "Connections — anyone you know who might help with grants, business advice, or introductions."

> "The business registration costs $50 and takes 15 minutes. That's the only thing standing between us and $275,000 in grant applications."

---

### Closing (30 seconds)

> "22 years of running 'The Conflux' as a name. Two years of job market rejection. All of it led here. Everything I've ever done — the business ideas, learning to code, cybersecurity, all of it — it merges at The Conflux."

> "This is the one."

---

---

## Tips for the Presenter

1. **Show, don't tell.** The app running is worth 1000 words.
2. **Lead with emotion, back with numbers.** Start with the story, end with the math.
3. **Don't oversell.** The product speaks for itself. Let them react.
4. **Be honest about risks.** "This could fail. But the product is built, the market is huge, and the path is clear."
5. **End on the $50 line.** "Fifty dollars and 15 minutes to unlock $275,000 in grants." That's the mic drop.
