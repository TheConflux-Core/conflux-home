# Growth Questions — The Creative Engine

> **Purpose:** Force lateral thinking. Prevent tunnel vision. Find the moves nobody else sees.
> **How it works:** One question rotates daily. Agents and Don reflect on it. Ideas go to `OPPORTUNITY_FEED.json`.
> **Rule:** No question is off-limits. The uncomfortable questions are the valuable ones.

---

## How to Use

- Each morning, ZigBot posts the next question in rotation.
- Any agent can reflect on it during idle time.
- If the question sparks an idea → add it to the opportunity feed.
- Weekly retrospective: What did this week's questions actually produce?

---

## The Questions

### Category: Competitive Dominance

1. **What are our competitors NOT doing that their customers wish they would?**
2. **If a competitor raised $50M tomorrow, what would they build? Can we build it first with $0?**
3. **Who is our biggest competitor that doesn't know they're our competitor yet?**
4. **What would make a competitor's users switch to us overnight?**
5. **What feature would make our product impossible to rip out once adopted?**

### Category: Revenue Acceleration

6. **What's something we already built that we could charge for separately?**
7. **If we had to double revenue in 30 days, what would we do?**
8. **Who has money and a problem we can solve, but doesn't know we exist?**
9. **What's the most expensive problem our users have that we're not solving yet?**
10. **Can we create a "done for you" tier that's 10x the price?**

### Category: User Delight

11. **What would make a user say "holy shit" when they first open Conflux Home?**
12. **What feature would users pay for but not think to ask for?**
13. **If our users could wave a magic wand, what would they wish for?**
14. **What's the #1 friction point in our onboarding right now?**
15. **What would make users tell their friends about us without being asked?**

### Category: Operational Leverage

16. **What are we doing manually that could be automated this week?**
17. **What process breaks if we 10x our user base tomorrow?**
18. **If we could only ship one thing this month, what would have the biggest impact?**
19. **What are we spending money on that we could build ourselves?**
20. **What task takes the most agent time that could be reduced by half?**

### Category: Market Creation

21. **What category could we define that doesn't exist yet?**
22. **What analogy would make a non-technical person instantly understand what we do?**
23. **What adjacent market has the same customers but doesn't compete with us?**
24. **If we were a platform, not a product — what would developers build on us?**
25. **What would The Conflux look like in 5 years if everything goes right?**

### Category: Blue Ocean

26. **What's an industry that's desperate for AI but has zero good tools?**
27. **What demographic is completely ignored by every AI product?**
28. **What problem do people solve with duct tape and spreadsheets that we could automate?**
29. **Where is the AI hype NOT matching the AI reality, and can we fill the gap?**
30. **What's a use case so obvious nobody's bothered to build it?**

### Category: Defensibility

31. **What data are we collecting that becomes more valuable over time?**
32. **What network effect can we create? (More users → more value for each user)**
33. **What would it take for a well-funded startup to replicate what we've built?**
34. **What integrations would make us the default choice in our niche?**
35. **What's our "switching cost" — how painful is it to leave us?**

### Category: The Billion-Dollar Question

36. **What's the one thing that, if true, would make The Conflux a billion-dollar company?**
37. **What do billion-dollar companies have in common that we're missing?**
38. **If we IPO'd today, what would the S-1 say? What would be embarrassing?**
39. **What would a $1B acquirer want from us? Do we have it?**
40. **If Don could only work on one thing for the next year, what should it be?**

### Category: Wildcard

41. **What's the craziest idea that might actually work?**
42. **If we partnered with [random company], what could we build?**
43. **What would our product look like if it was designed for kids? For elderly? For non-English speakers?**
44. **If our servers went down for a week, what would users miss most? That's our core.**
45. **What would we build if money and time were no object? Can we start now?**
