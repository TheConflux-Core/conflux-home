## The Five Divisions

### 🧠 STRATEGY DIVISION
*Where we're going and why.*

| Agent | Role | Decides |
|-------|------|---------|
| **ZigBot** | Strategic Partner | Helps Don think, prioritize, frame decisions |
| **Vector** | Chief Business Strategist | APPROVE / REJECT / REFINE every opportunity |

**How they grow the empire:**
- ZigBot identifies promising opportunities from market signals, Don's insights, and portfolio analysis
- Vector applies rigorous financial/strategic review before anything enters the pipeline
- Together they ensure the company never wastes resources on low-value work
- They maintain strategic focus — one thing done well beats ten things done poorly

**Revenue impact:** Every approved opportunity should have a clear path to revenue. Vector won't approve anything without unit economics.

---

### 🔴 SECURITY DIVISION
*Protecting everything we build.*

| Agent | Role | Decides |
|-------|------|---------|
| **Viper** 🐍 | Red Team Operator | What's vulnerable, what needs fixing |
| **Aegis** 🛡️ | Blue Team Guardian | How to defend, what to harden, what to monitor |

**How they grow the empire:**
- **Trust = Revenue.** Users won't pay for an app that leaks their data. Security is a feature, not overhead.
- Viper finds → Aegis fixes. The cycle prevents the catastrophic breach that kills a startup.
- Pre-commit hooks stop API key leaks (we've been burned — never again).
- Security reviews before every release protect the brand.
- Compliance readiness (GDPR, CCPA) unlocks international markets.

**Revenue impact:** Security enables enterprise deals, international expansion, and premium trust tiers. "Conflux Home is secure by design" is a marketing message Pulse can sell.

### ⚙️ PRODUCT DIVISION
*Building what users pay for.*

| Agent | Role | Decides |
|-------|------|---------|
| **Forge** 🔨 | Execution & Builder | What to build, how to build it |
| **Quanta** ✓ | Verification & QA | What's ready to ship, what's broken |
| **Pulse** 📣 | Growth & Marketing | How to reach users, how to convert them |

**How they grow the empire:**

**Forge** is the factory. 251+ Rust commands, 91+ DB tables, 15 apps built in days. Forge turns approved ideas into real product. Speed of building = speed of iteration = speed of finding product-market fit.

**Pulse** is the growth engine. SEO, social, content, launch strategy, creative testing, audience acquisition. Pulse turns product into attention, attention into downloads, downloads into revenue.

**Revenue impact:** This is the core value chain. Build → Verify → Market → Revenue. Pulse is the only agent directly responsible for user acquisition.

## How Work Flows — From Idea to Revenue

```
1. DISCOVER
   Helix researches market → finds opportunity
   ZigBot frames opportunity for Don → strategic clarity
                          ↓
2. DECIDE
   Vector evaluates: revenue potential, build cost, market timing
   Returns: APPROVE / REJECT / REFINE
                          ↓
3. PLAN
   Prism creates mission record → canonical state
   Spectra decomposes into tasks → buildable pieces
                          ↓
4. BUILD
   Luma launches runs → dispatches workers
   Forge builds artifacts → code, configs, assets
   Helix provides research → competitive context
   Bolt ensures it builds → CI/CD integration
                          ↓
5. PROTECT
   Viper scans for vulnerabilities → findings
   Aegis hardens architecture → defenses
   Lex reviews legal surface → compliance
   Ledger validates unit economics → profitability
                          ↓
6. VERIFY
   Quanta checks everything → quality gate
   Nothing ships without Quanta's sign-off
                          ↓
7. LAUNCH
   Pulse creates launch assets → SEO, social, content
   Bolt handles release pipeline → builds, signs, deploys
   Ledger tracks first revenue → Stripe live
                          ↓
8. GROW
   Pulse drives traffic → downloads, users, attention
   Ledger monitors metrics → CAC, LTV, churn
   Helix watches market → adapts strategy
   ZigBot + Vector decide next move → repeat
```

## Autonomous Operations — How It Works Without Don

The goal is for Don to be a **strategic director**, not a daily operator.

**Daily (fully autonomous):**
- Bolt checks CI/CD health → alerts on failure
- Viper scans for new vulnerabilities → reports findings
- Aegis monitors security posture → reviews Viper's findings
- Ledger tracks API costs → flags anomalies
- Pulse publishes scheduled content → monitors engagement

**Weekly (Don reviews):**
- Prism summarizes mission status → blocked items, completed work
- Ledger provides burn report → costs, revenue, runway
- Helix delivers market brief → competitive moves, opportunities
- Pulse reports growth metrics → downloads, users, conversion

**Monthly (Don decides):**
- Vector evaluates portfolio → approve/reject new initiatives
- ZigBot frames strategic options → what to prioritize next
- Ledger presents financial review → is the math working?
- Lex checks compliance posture → any regulatory changes?

**Quarterly (Don directs):**
- Full team retrospective → what worked, what didn't
- Strategic pivot assessment → stay the course or adapt?
- Roadmap planning → next quarter's priorities
- Budget allocation → where to invest next

## The Secret Sauce

What makes this different from a regular software company:

1. **16 agents, not 16 employees.** No salaries, no benefits, no office space. Scales linearly with compute, not headcount.

2. **Canonical state, not meetings.** Agents coordinate through files, not Slack threads. Decisions are recorded, not forgotten.

3. **Anti-hallucination protocol.** Every agent verifies before reporting. No "I think the build passed." No "probably fine."

4. **Don is the vision, agents are the execution.** Don doesn't write code, manage CI/CD, or draft ToS. He directs the orchestra.

5. **The engine IS the product.** The multi-agent orchestration system running The Conflux is the same engine inside Conflux Home. We eat our own dog food.

6. **Audio background = unfair advantage.** No tech founder has Don's professional audio experience. The AI record label play is unopposed.
