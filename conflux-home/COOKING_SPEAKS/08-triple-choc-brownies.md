# 🍫 Triple Chocolate Brownies

**Category:** Dessert
**Prep Time:** 15 min
**Cook Time:** 25 min
**Servings:** 12
**Difficulty:** Easy

## 🧾 Ingredients
- ½ cup butter
- 4 oz dark chocolate (chopped)
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 eggs
- ⅓ cup cocoa powder
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- Pinch of salt
- ½ cup milk chocolate chips
- ½ cup white chocolate chips

## 👨‍🍳 Steps

### Step 1 — Melt & Mix
> *"Melt butter and dark chocolate together. Stir in sugar, then whisk in eggs one at a time."*

Melt butter and dark chocolate together in a microwave (30-second intervals) or double boiler. Stir until smooth. Mix in sugar, then whisk in eggs one at a time until glossy.

### Step 2 — Fold & Pour
> *"Fold in cocoa powder, flour, and a pinch of salt. Pour into a greased baking pan."*

Sift in cocoa powder, flour, and salt. Fold gently until just combined (don't overmix). Pour into a greased 9x9 baking pan.

### Step 3 — Top & Bake
> *"Sprinkle milk and white chocolate chips on top. Bake at 350 for twenty five minutes. Let cool before cutting."*

Sprinkle milk chocolate and white chocolate chips evenly over the top. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 25 minutes. The center should be slightly fudgy. Let cool for at least 15 minutes before cutting.

## 💡 Pro Tips
- **Don't overbake!** Pull them when the center still looks slightly underdone — they firm up as they cool.
- **Use quality chocolate** — it makes a huge difference.
- **Add sea salt flakes** on top before baking for salted brownies.
