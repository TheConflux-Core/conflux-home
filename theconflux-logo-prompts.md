## Recommended Generation Order

1. **Prompt 2 (Icon)** — Get the icon mark first. Everything else builds from this.
2. **Prompt 1 (Primary Logo)** — Full logo with wordmark.
3. **Prompt 3 (Abstract)** — The wow piece for the hero.
4. **Prompt 5 (Social Banner)** — Quick win for social presence.
5. **Prompt 6 (OG Image)** — Needed for website launch.
6. **Prompt 4 (Letterhead)** — For consulting docs.
7. **Prompt 7 (Animation Reference)** — For when we build the site.

---

## Generation Tips

- **Best tools for logos:** Ideogram (text rendering), Midjourney (aesthetics), DALL-E (control)
- **Generate 4+ variations** of each prompt, pick the best
- **Icon must work at 16x16** — if it doesn't read small, simplify it
- **Test on dark AND light backgrounds** — some logos break on one
- **Keep the icon geometric** — organic shapes don't scale well
