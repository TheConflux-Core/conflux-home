# 🌶️ Jalapeño Popper Sliders

**Category:** Main Dish
**Prep Time:** 15 min
**Cook Time:** 10 min
**Servings:** 12 sliders
**Difficulty:** Easy

## 🧾 Ingredients
- 1.5 lbs ground beef
- 12 slider buns
- 4 oz cream cheese (softened)
- 2 jalapeños (diced, seeds removed for less heat)
- 6 slices pepper jack cheese (halved)
- 6 strips bacon (cooked crispy, halved)
- Salt, pepper, garlic powder

## 👨‍🍳 Steps

### Step 1 — Form the Patties
> *"Form your ground beef into small slider patties. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder."*

Divide ground beef into 12 equal portions. Form into small patties slightly larger than the buns (they'll shrink). Season both sides with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.

### Step 2 — Grill the Patties
> *"Grill the patties three minutes per side. In a bowl, mix cream cheese with diced jalapeños."*

Grill patties over medium-high heat for 3 minutes per side. While cooking, mix softened cream cheese with diced jalapeños in a small bowl.

### Step 3 — Assemble
> *"Spread the jalapeño cream cheese on each slider bun. Add the patty, a slice of pepper jack, and crispy bacon."*

Spread jalapeño cream cheese on the bottom bun. Add the cooked patty, half a slice of pepper jack cheese, and half a strip of crispy bacon. Top with the bun.

## 💡 Pro Tips
- **Toast the buns** on the grill for 30 seconds for extra crunch.
- **Bacon shortcut:** Use pre-cooked bacon, microwaved until crispy.
- **Keep seeds** in the jalapeños if you like it hot.
