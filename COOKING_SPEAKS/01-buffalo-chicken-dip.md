# 🐔 Buffalo Chicken Dip

## 🧾 Ingredients
- 2 chicken breasts
- 8 oz cream cheese (softened)
- ½ cup ranch dressing
- ½ cup hot sauce (Frank's RedHot recommended)
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- Tortilla chips (for serving)

## 👨‍🍳 Steps

### Step 1 — Shred the Chicken
> *"Start by boiling your chicken breasts until fully cooked. Shred them up nice and fine."*

Boil chicken breasts in salted water for 15–18 minutes until cooked through. Remove, let cool slightly, then shred with two forks.

### Step 2 — Mix the Base
> *"Mix the shredded chicken with cream cheese, ranch dressing, and hot sauce. Spread it in a baking dish."*

In a large bowl, combine shredded chicken, softened cream cheese, ranch dressing, and hot sauce. Stir until evenly mixed. Spread into a 9x9 baking dish.

### Step 3 — Bake & Serve
> *"Top with shredded cheddar and bake at 350 for twenty minutes. Serve warm with tortilla chips."*

Sprinkle shredded cheddar on top. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 20 minutes until bubbly and golden. Serve warm with tortilla chips.

## 💡 Pro Tips
- **Make it ahead:** Assemble the night before, refrigerate, then bake when ready.
- **Spice level:** Adjust hot sauce to taste — start with ¼ cup for mild.
- **Shredded rotisserie chicken** works great as a shortcut.
