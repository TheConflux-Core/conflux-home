## 🧾 Ingredients
- 2 cups watermelon (diced)
- 1 cup strawberries (diced)
- 1 mango (diced)
- ½ cup blueberries
- 2 tbsp honey
- Juice of 2 limes
- ¼ tsp chili powder (optional but recommended)

## 👨‍🍳 Steps

### Step 1 — Dice the Fruit
> *"Dice watermelon, strawberries, and mango into bite-size chunks. Add blueberries and toss gently."*

Cut watermelon, strawberries, and mango into uniform bite-size chunks. Add to a large bowl with the blueberries. Toss gently to combine.

### Step 2 — Make the Dressing
> *"Whisk together honey, lime juice, and a pinch of chili powder. Drizzle over the fruit."*

In a small bowl, whisk together honey, fresh lime juice, and chili powder until smooth. Drizzle over the fruit.

### Step 3 — Toss & Chill
> *"Toss gently and chill for ten minutes. The sweet heat combo is amazing."*

Toss the fruit gently with the dressing. Refrigerate for at least 10 minutes before serving.

## 💡 Pro Tips
- **Chili powder + lime + honey** = Mexican street fruit magic. Don't skip it.
- **Tajín seasoning** is a great substitute for chili powder.
- **Add cucumber** for extra crunch and freshness.
