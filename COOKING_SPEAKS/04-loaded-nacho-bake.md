# 🧀 Loaded Nacho Bake

**Category:** Main Dish
**Prep Time:** 10 min
**Cook Time:** 12 min
**Servings:** 6
**Difficulty:** Easy

## 🧾 Ingredients
- 1 large bag tortilla chips
- 1 lb ground beef (seasoned with taco seasoning)
- 1 can black beans (drained)
- 1 cup corn kernels
- 2 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend
- ½ cup sour cream
- ½ cup salsa
- 2 jalapeños (sliced)
- Fresh cilantro (chopped)

## 👨‍🍳 Steps

### Step 1 — Layer the Base
> *"Layer tortilla chips on a sheet pan. Top with seasoned ground beef, black beans, and corn."*

Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Spread tortilla chips in an even layer on a large sheet pan. Brown the ground beef with taco seasoning, then scatter over the chips. Add black beans and corn.

### Step 2 — Melt the Cheese
> *"Cover with shredded cheese and bake at 375 until bubbly. About ten minutes."*

Cover everything with shredded Mexican cheese blend. Bake for 10 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

### Step 3 — Load the Toppings
> *"Finish with sour cream, salsa, sliced jalapeños, and fresh cilantro. Dig in!"*

Remove from oven. Dollop sour cream over the top, drizzle with salsa, add sliced jalapeños, and garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve immediately.

## 💡 Pro Tips
- **Use a silpat or parchment** on the sheet pan for easy cleanup.
- **Swap ground beef** for shredded chicken or carnitas.
- **Add guacamole** on the side for the full experience.
