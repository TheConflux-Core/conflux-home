## 🧾 Ingredients
- 2–3 scoops vanilla ice cream
- 1 can Coke Zero (cold)
- 1 maraschino cherry
- Whipped cream (optional)

## 👨‍🍳 Steps

### Step 1 — Scoop & Pour
> *"Scoop vanilla ice cream into a tall glass. Slowly pour Coke Zero over the top."*

Scoop 2–3 generous scoops of vanilla ice cream into a tall glass. Slowly pour cold Coke Zero over the ice cream — pour slowly to control the fizz.

### Step 2 — Top & Enjoy
> *"Add a straw, a cherry on top, and enjoy the classic float with a zero sugar twist!"*

Add a straw, top with a maraschino cherry (and whipped cream if you're feeling fancy). Serve immediately while it's still fizzy and foamy.

## 💡 Pro Tips
- **Chill the glass** in the freezer for 10 minutes first for maximum frost.
- **Use a spoon-straw** — you'll need both.
- **Try different Coke Zero flavors** — Cherry or Vanilla Coke Zero are 🔥.
