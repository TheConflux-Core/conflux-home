## 🧾 Ingredients
- 3 cups watermelon (cubed, seedless)
- 1 cup ice
- Juice of 1 lime
- 8–10 fresh mint leaves
- ½ cup sparkling water
- Mint sprig & lime wheel (for garnish)

## 👨‍🍳 Steps

### Step 1 — Blend
> *"Blend cubed watermelon with ice, lime juice, and a handful of fresh mint leaves."*

Add watermelon cubes, ice, lime juice, and mint leaves to a blender. Blend on high until smooth (about 30 seconds).

### Step 2 — Strain & Serve
> *"Strain into a glass and top with sparkling water. Garnish with a mint sprig and lime wheel."*

Strain through a fine mesh sieve into glasses (optional — skip if you like the pulp). Top with sparkling water. Garnish with a fresh mint sprig and lime wheel.

## 💡 Pro Tips
- **Frozen watermelon** makes it thicker and colder — skip the ice.
- **Add vodka or rum** for an adult version.
- **Muddle the mint** before blending for stronger flavor.
