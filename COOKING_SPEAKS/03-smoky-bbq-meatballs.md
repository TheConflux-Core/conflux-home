# 🍖 Smoky BBQ Meatballs

**Category:** Main Dish
**Prep Time:** 15 min
**Cook Time:** 20 min
**Servings:** 6
**Difficulty:** Easy

## 🧾 Ingredients
- 2 lbs ground beef
- ½ cup breadcrumbs
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- Salt & pepper to taste
- 1.5 cups BBQ sauce (your favorite)
- 2 green onions (sliced, for garnish)

## 👨‍🍳 Steps

### Step 1 — Mix & Roll
> *"Mix ground beef with breadcrumbs, egg, and your favorite seasoning. Roll into one-inch balls."*

In a large bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix gently with your hands. Roll into 1-inch meatballs (should make about 30).

### Step 2 — Brown & Simmer
> *"Brown the meatballs in a skillet, then simmer in barbecue sauce for fifteen minutes."*

Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Brown the meatballs on all sides (about 5 minutes). Pour BBQ sauce over the meatballs, reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer for 15 minutes, turning occasionally.

### Step 3 — Garnish & Serve
> *"Garnish with sliced green onions and serve with toothpicks. These are a crowd favorite!"*

Transfer to a serving dish. Garnish with sliced green onions. Insert toothpicks for easy serving.

## 💡 Pro Tips
- **Oven method:** Bake at 400°F for 20 minutes, then toss in BBQ sauce.
- **Mix BBQ sauces** — half sweet, half smoky for depth.
- **Make ahead:** Freeze uncooked meatballs on a sheet pan, then bag them. Cook from frozen (add 10 min).
