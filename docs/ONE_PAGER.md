# Conflux Home

### A home for your AI family.

---

## The Problem

AI is powerful. But for most people, it's still just a chat window.

There's no product that gives everyday people a **team of AI agents** that actually know them — their budget, their kitchen, their goals, their life. No product that feels like *yours* instead of *theirs*.

## The Solution

**Conflux Home** is a desktop application that gives you a pre-configured team of AI agents with personalities, memory, and purpose — out of the box.

Download → Create an account → Your team is alive. Two minutes from install to working.

### What You Get

**16 built-in apps**, each powered by a specialized AI agent:

| App | What It Does |
|-----|-------------|
| 🍳 **Kitchen (Hearth)** | Meal planning, pantry tracking, grocery lists — knows what you have and what you can make |
| 💰 **Budget (Pulse)** | Natural language expense tracking, pattern detection, savings goals |
| 🧠 **Life Autopilot (Orbit)** | Focus engine, habit tracking, smart rescheduling |
| 🎯 **Dreams (Horizon)** | Goal decomposition, milestone tracking, velocity metrics |
| 📡 **Feed (Current)** | Daily briefing, ripple radar, signal threads |
| 🏠 **Home (Foundation)** | Maintenance tracking, appliance monitoring, seasonal tasks |
| 📁 **Vault** | File browser, project organization, tag system |
| 🎨 **Studio** | Image generation, voice synthesis, creative tools |
| 💬 **Echo** | Communication hub with pattern recognition |
| 🎮 **Games** | Minesweeper, Snake, Pac-Man, Solitaire |
| 🤖 **Agent Marketplace** | Discover and install new agents and skills |

### The Agents

Each app is powered by a named agent with a defined personality and purpose:

- **Conflux** — Your strategic partner
- **Hearth** — Kitchen & nutrition intelligence
- **Pulse** — Financial awareness
- **Orbit** — Life management
- **Horizon** — Dream & goal tracking
- **Current** — Information curation

You don't talk to "the AI." You talk to *your team*.
